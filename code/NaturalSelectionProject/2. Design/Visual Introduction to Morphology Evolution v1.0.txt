Natural Selection iPhone Application
Visual Introduction to Morphology Evolution, v1.0
--------------------------------------------------
Jason Brownlee, March 2009

Introduction
--------------------------------------------------
This document provides a visual introduction to the morphology evolution via the use of web page references. Specifically, images and videos are presented that exemplify the inspiration for the Natural Selection project.

Ideas
--------------------------------------------------
This section provides free-form ideas collated while watching the videos listed in this document.
	- process co-evolves virtual creatures brains and bodies
	- three dimensional virtual creature evolution, alife
	- provide a collection of pre-fabricated general-purpose creatures as a base for adaptation (worm, etc)
	- allow the user to pick up, drop, throw creatures in the environment (play mode)
	- use cloud speed in the sky to provide impression of time passing, and time passing faster (2X, 4X, ...)
	- enforce constraints on the process, such as bilateral symmetry and maximum number of blocks
	- use fog to give the impression of different environments (under water, in the sky)
	- stretch a textured skin/mesh over the polygons to give a more organic look and feel
	- include sound effects for ground touching
	- use an oscillator to get repetitive movements
